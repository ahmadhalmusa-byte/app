App name: ScanFlow - Barcode & QR Scanner Short description: Powerful offline barcode & QR code scanner with scan history Full description: ScanFlow is a modern Android barcode and QR code scanner designed for productivity and speed. Features fast on-device scanning using ML Kit technology, supports QR Code, Code 128, EAN-13, and UPC-A. Scan history is saved locally - never lose your scans. Organize with categories, add notes, search and filter, star favorites. Batch scanning mode for inventory. Export to CSV. Modern Material Design 3 interface with dark mode. Arabic and English language. No internet required. Category: Tools → Utilities Tags: barcode, qr, scanner, offline, productivity, inventory
